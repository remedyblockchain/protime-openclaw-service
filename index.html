What We Eliminate
The problems that kill every DIY AI setup.

Most business owners who try to self-configure an AI agent system hit the same walls. We've seen them all — and we handle every one.

The blank terminal problem.

Linux configs, Node version conflicts, gateway settings, environment variables — before you've built a single agent. Most non-technical owners quit here or get stuck for weeks.

API key chaos.

Juggling 8–12 integrations means OAuth flows, token expiry, scope misconfigurations, and credentials stored in the wrong place. One expired token and your agent silently stops working.

The "it worked yesterday" problem.

Cron jobs that silently fail. Webhook endpoints that break when URLs rotate. Agent memory that goes stale. Without proactive monitoring, you won't know something's broken until it costs you.

Generic agents that don't sound like you.

Getting an AI agent to actually reflect your voice, mission, and operating rules requires deep identity design. Without it, every output feels off — and trust in the system erodes fast.

AI spend with no visibility.

Without model routing, every task hits the most expensive model by default. Costs compound fast and outputs aren't even better. Smart routing cuts waste without sacrificing quality.

50+ hours and nothing to show.

The real cost isn't the setup hours — it's the delay in what the system enables. Every week spent debugging is a week your AI isn't running outreach, monitoring opportunities, or handling ops.
